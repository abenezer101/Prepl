## 1. Product Overview

**Prepl** is an AI-native, autonomous recruitment and interview intelligence platform. It functions as an **Autonomous HR Co-Founder** for early-stage startups and technical founders, transforming the traditionally bottlenecked hiring pipeline into a self-operating, 24/7 intelligence system. 

Unlike traditional static ATS software or primitive interview wrappers, Prepl coordinates a cohort of specialized AI agents running on production autopilot. It ingests complex inbound application flows, configures tailored evaluation sandboxes, hosts real-time multimodal technical screening interviews, and generates structured hiring analytics. 

Simultaneously, Prepl deploys an integrated candidate-side ecosystem. When candidates apply via an employer's custom Prepl link, they unlock a localized "Practice Warmup Mode," allowing them to run diagnostic mock interviews mapped to the exact position before entering the live evaluation channel. The entire system operates natively on Google Cloud infrastructure utilizing the advanced multimodal capabilities of the Gemini API.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

### For Founders & Early-Stage Startups (B2B)

The single biggest bottleneck preventing micro-enterprises and technical startups from scaling and generating new jobs is the immense operational time drain of recruitment. Technical founders are forced to spend dozens of hours manually scanning resumes, parsing disparate GitHub profiles, and running introductory screening calls. Human bias, inconsistent baseline metrics, and the prohibitive cost of dedicated recruiters result in delayed hiring cycles, frozen growth, and high friction in team development.

### For Job Seekers & Recent Graduates (B2C)

Entering a competitive job market induces massive anxiety. Traditional prep methods rely on generic, static question banks (e.g., LeetCode, Glassdoor) that fail to simulate the unique, conversational, non-scripted fluid dynamics of a modern technical interview. Candidates lack access to personalized, real-time behavioral insights regarding *how* they communicate, cross-referenced with *what* they know technically—leaving them disadvantaged during live evaluations.

---

## 3. Solution: The Autonomous Recruiting Flywheel

Prepl resolves both bottlenecks simultaneously by shifting the product architecture into a unified B2B2C operational flywheel:

* **The Revenue Engine (B2B):** Provides technical founders with a completely autonomous AI recruiting layer that processes applicant profiles, evaluates skills through sandboxed challenges, and runs live, conversational, behaviorally aware video screenings. 
* **The Viral Acquisition Engine (B2C):** Embeds an organic candidate-side practice portal directly inside the application stream. Candidates receive top-tier, personalized preparation, removing interview anxiety while feeding clean, deeply analyzed data directly into the employer's dashboard.

By decreasing the operational overhead of hiring to near zero, Prepl allows new startups to aggressively scale their workforce, driving economic output and job creation.

## 5. Core Features — The Autonomous Recruiting Engine (v1 B2B)

### 5.1 Autonomous Sourcing & Profile Ingestion

Employers drop raw inbound application data—including Resumes, CVs, LinkedIn URLs, GitHub profiles, and project portfolios—directly into Prepl. The platform’s ingestion layer decomposes the data, analyzing historical code repositories, architecture decisions, and operational histories against the target Job Description (JD).

### 5.2 Sandbox Isolation & Challenge Synthesis

The platform dynamically designs tailored evaluation tasks based on the specific role profile. For technical roles, it constructs isolated computing environments where candidates can walk through live engineering architecture problems, enabling the AI to evaluate technical reasoning, code patterns, and systems logic on the fly.

### 5.3 Live Autonomous Screener Agent

An AI recruiting agent opens the live assessment window, guides the candidate through authentication, and conducts a dynamic, conversational voice/video interview. The agent adapts question pathways mid-session based on the candidate's technical assertions, deep-dives into vague responses, and evaluates operational problem-solving capacity in real time.

---

## 6. Core Features — The Candidate Warmup Platform (v1 B2C)

### 6.1 Personalized Diagnostic Mock Sessions

Before stepping into an official employer screening, candidates use Prepl's candidate channel to execute practice sessions. The agent synthesizes mock tracks by merging the candidate's personal portfolio context directly with the real-world operational parameters of the targeted job opening.

### 6.2 Real-Time Conversational Adaptation

The candidate interacts with a highly articulate, responsive AI interviewer that maintains context over an extended technical dialogue. The model challenges assumptions, requires explanations for technical trade-offs, and mimics a high-stakes team matching discussion.

## 9. Universal Accessibility Suite

To ensure absolute neutrality and equal accessibility during autonomous corporate screening, Prepl deploys a robust accessibility core across all tiers from day one:

* **Multimodal Speech Synthesis:** Implements fluid, real-time Text-to-Speech (TTS) pipelines for visually impaired users.
* **Semantic Transcription & Captioning:** Employs ultra-low latency caption generation tracking all verbal interactions instantly.
* **Screen Reader Optimization:** Structured strictly according to ARIA guidelines to allow seamless keyboard navigation and screen reader parsing.
* **Sign Language Layout Support:** Built-in modular frame adjustments to natively accommodate sign language video inputs and spatial tracking overlays.
